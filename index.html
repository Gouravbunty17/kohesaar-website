About Us

About KOHESAAR

Incorporated under the Ontario Business Corporations Act in 2017, KOHESAAR GLOBAL MANAGEMENT INC. operates as a private investment and B2B distribution company with the flexibility to run multi-category trade programs across North America.

We partner with verified suppliers, manufacturers and logistics providers to build reliable routes into Canada and the United States. Our team focuses on sectors where we understand manufacturing bases, quality expectations and regulatory requirements.

KOHESAAR deploys its own capital into commercial ventures, inventory, and supply-chain opportunities that align with a long-term, relationship-driven strategy. We are not a broker of other people's money — we stand behind the decisions we make.

  • Legal form Ontario Business Corporation (No. 2590129)
  • Jurisdiction Province of Ontario, Canada
  • Core markets Canada & United States (North America)
  • Clients Manufacturers, brand owners, distributors & B2B buyers
  • Capital Company-owned; no public advisory or portfolio management

Our Approach

Our Process

Structured approach to building scalable, compliant B2B programs.

1

Discovery & Assessment

Initial consultation to understand your products, markets, and objectives. We evaluate alignment with our operational capabilities and investment criteria.

2

Feasibility Analysis

Market research, regulatory review, and supply-chain assessment across Canada & U.S. We identify potential barriers and develop mitigation strategies.

3

Structured pilots with limited SKUs, controlled volumes, and documented performance. Real-world validation before full commitment.

4

Scale & Optimize

Scale-up of successful programs with continuous improvement and risk management. Long-term partnership development and expansion.

Our Philosophy

How We Work

KOHESAAR operates with a long-term, relationship-focused approach that prioritizes transparency, practicality and compliance. Programs start with structured pilots before any scale-up.

Integrity

Direct, transparent communication. Clear contracts, realistic expectations and zero tolerance for hidden terms.

Reliability

Consistent fills, predictable lead times and stable quality over chasing the absolute lowest-cost supplier.

Partnership

Long-term relationships with manufacturers and buyers matter more than one-off wins.

Compliance

Operations structured around applicable Canadian and U.S. trade, customs and commercial regulations.

Discipline

Capital and capacity committed only where risk-return and operational fit make sense.

Long-Term View

All decisions taken with a multi-year horizon: reputational impact, regulatory safety and durability.
